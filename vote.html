Why voting for Autonio ?

Autonio is one of 14 projects that were selected in the current round of voting at Ethfinex exchange. Community members can vote or support the listing campaign on social media through our links to participate. Faq

What is this vote all about?

The vote is for the Ethfinex listing competition, which ends on
August 21st 2018.

How much does EVT cost?

EVT tokens are openly traded on Ethfinex exchange at a price set by the market. At time of publication EVT tokens were trading at $0.0043990 USD. Since demand will impact price, if you want to purchase EVT we suggest using limit orders rather than market buying.

Why should I participate?

Ethfinex exchange is one of the larger decentralized exchanges, with an average volume placing it in the top 100 exchanges overall.

What happens if I participate in the vote and Autonio doesn’t finish in the top three entries or is not listed on Ethfinex?

The refunds will still be issued in that case. Refunds will be issued in NIO and pegged to an equivalent amount USD spent by community members on the EVT vote at the time of refund.

How do I vote?

You need an Ethfinex account to on their exchange and purchase EVT voting tokens. From there you can transfer your EVT tokens to Metamask or Ledger and visit https://nectar.community/#/listings to vote for Autonio.

How will I receive the voting rewards?

Simply fill out this form and save your transaction id from the vote for your records. Our team will go through the submissions and process your reward after the vote is concluded.

When does the vote end?

Tuesday, August 21st 17:40 UTC Time. Please submit your final votes at least an hour before the deadline to allow for any delays in the ETH network. A countdown for the end of the vote can be found here.
